1. What if you forget the Account Root User password? [AWS: General, AWS Account, Permissions]
    1. If you are a root user and you lost or forgot the root password, you can reset your password.
    2. To reset your password, go to the AWS Management Console, enter your AWS root account email address, click Next
    3. Choose Forgot your password?
    4. Provide your AWS account root users' email address, click Continue.
    5. Check your email for a reply from a valid @amazon.com, or @aws.amazon.com account, and follow the instructions in that email.

1. Is AWS one company globally? [AWS: General: Miscellaneous]
    1. AWS or Amazon Web Services is a wholly owned subsidiaries of Amazon.com, Inc.
    2. I suspect your question is more about whether every AWS Region is under the same tax, marketing, or legal entity? I'm not a lawyer, or accountant and I won't try to speak to that, however AWS sells cloud services to customers globally via various Sellers of Record (SoRs).
    3. As far as I can determine, the services available to AWS customers is not dependent on the AWS account's SOR.
    4. Here are the SoRs that I'm aware of:
      1. AWS, Inc.
        1. US-based
        2. Coverage includes: most of the Americas, the Caribbean, and most of Asia Pacific.
      2. AWS EMEA
        1. Based in Luxembourg
        2. Coverage: EMEA - Europe, Middle East, and most of Africa
      3. AWS South Africa
        1. Based in South Africa
        2. Coverage: South Africa
      4. AWS AISPL
        1. AISPL (Amazon Internet Services Private Limited)
        2. Based in India
        3. Coverage: India
      5. AWS Brazil
        1. Based in Brazil
        2. Coverage: Brazil
      6. AWS Korea
        1. Based in Korea
        2. Coverage: South Korea
      7. Beijing Sinnet technology Co., Ltd. And Ningxia Western Cloud Technology Co., Ltd
        1. Third-party companies
        2. Based in China
        3. Coverage: China

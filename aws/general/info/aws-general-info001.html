1. What are the top cloud services? [AWS: General: Information]
    1. The top cloud services across the major hyperscalers changes over time
    2. Based on research done by Flexera, service usage beyond the core compute, storage and network services continues to grow
    3. In their 2022 State of the Cloud Report, Flexera highlighted the following as the top three public cloud services in use by organizations:
      1. Data warehouse
      2. Relational database-as-a-service (DBaaS)
      3. Container-as-a-service
    4. They also found that organizations were experimenting with Serverless, ML/AI (Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence), and IoT

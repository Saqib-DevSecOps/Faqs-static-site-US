1. Will I see my score immediately after completing the exam? [AWS: General: Certifications]
    1. It depends on which AWS Certification Exam you take
    2. You will see your pass/fail status upon completing the following exams:
      1. AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner
      2. AWS Certified Machine Learning - Specialty
      3. AWS Certified: SAP on AWS - Specialty exams
    3. The detailed exam results will be available in your AWS Certification Account, up to five business days after the exam.
    4. For all other exams, you will get a detailed exam result (with your score) within five business days after the exam
    5. References:
      1. AWS Certification FAQ

1. What if I miss an AWS training class, can I access the recording? [AWS: General: Certifications]
    1. AWS Training does not record their live sessions, however you can register for another session of the AWS Partner: AWS Cloud Practitioner Essentials class.
    2. You can go to the Partner Course Schedule page for a list of our classes, or directly search the foundational courses here.
    3. Additionally you can access a recording via the digital version of the course.
    4. In all cases, be sure to sign-in with your AWS Partner PartnerCentral account.

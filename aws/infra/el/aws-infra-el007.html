1. What are called Edge Location? [AWS, Infrastructure, AWS Edge Locations]
    1. Edge Locations can be used to cache content, and cache content in a way that the content is located closer to the end-user
    2. That way, the end-user can access the content with lower latency, than if the content were located in the AWS Region
    3. Content providers such as Amazon Prime or a web site administrator might choose to cache a copy of their content in an Edge Location, closer to the end-user

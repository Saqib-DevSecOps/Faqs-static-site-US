1. Can I pre-populate content at the Edge Locations? [AWS: Infrastructure: AWS Edge Locations]
    1. Not that I know of
    2. However consider if this is the right approach. I.e. why do you want to pre-populate content? Is there a better way to meet the need?
    3. Considerations:
      1. The initial access will result in the content being pulled from the Origin server into the Edge Location
      2. CloudFront maintains a pool of persistent connections to the Origin server and the communication is over the AWS backbone, not the Internet, improving latency
      3. If the pre-populated content is not accessed for a specific amount of time, or if it needs to make room for other more active content, CloudFront will automatically de-stage inactive content

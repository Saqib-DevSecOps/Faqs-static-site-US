1. Is it possible for 2 Availability Zones to go down at the same time? [AWS, Infrastructure, AWS Availability Zone]
    1. Possible, however not probable
    2. Availability Zones (AZs)are a high availability feature in AWS
    3. Every current AWS Region has at least 3 AZs. Depending on the business criticality, customer can choose to deploy across two or more AZs
    4. Also AZs are located up to 100 KM or 60 miles from each other. Chances of, for example a natural disaster affecting two AZs at the same time are low
    5. Organizations should design their high-availability architectures to meet their business and mission needs, including planning for cross-region HA if appropriate

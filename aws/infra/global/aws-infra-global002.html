    3. AWS China Region
      1. There are two Regions in China:
        1. AWS China (Beijing) Region
          1. based out of Beijing and adjacent area
          2. managed by service provider Beijing Sinnet Technology Co., Ltd. (Sinnet).
          3. Region code: cn-north-1.
        2. AWS China (Ningxia) Region
          1. based out of Ningxia
          2. managed by service provider Ningxia Western Cloud Data Technology Co., Ltd. (NWCD)
          3. Region code: cn-northwest-1.
      2. These are the AWS-cn partitions
      1. Note: AWS provides a full, and robust complement of Services in the China Regions, however there are some notable differences from the rest of the AWS commercial Regions to be aware of, including:
        1. No direct connection to the AWS backbone
        2. Management: "AWS has collaborated with local partners in China who have proper telecom licenses for delivering cloud services within the AWS China (Beijing) Region and the AWS China (Ningxia) Region."
        3. Trademark restriction on "AWS". The Beijing Municipal People's Court ruled that the "AWS" trademark belongs to a local firm, ActionSoft Science & Technology Development Co.
      1. "Customers who wish to use the China Regions are required to sign up for a separate set of account credentials unique to China services."
      2. "Only China-based and multinational companies with customers in China can apply for an account."

    4. AWS GovCloud US
      1. AWS GovCloud is a secured AWS region built for United States government agencies and companies that work with and support them.
      2. AWS GovCloud is for US-based public sector customers, government agencies and its contractors
      3. AWS GovCloud operates alongside the Top Secret and Secret Regions that support the intelligence community and other agencies who store classified information.
      4. "Access to the GovCloud region is restricted to a vetted set of USA-based individuals. Its servers are situated on United States soil, and it is managed and run only by United States citizens."
      5. There are two Regions in US Government Cloud, e.g. GovCloud West, us-gov-west-1, and GovCloud East, us-gov-east-1
      6. Have partition name AWS-us-gov.
      1. References:
        1. AWS GovCloud Services: Sensitive and Classified Data on the Public Cloud
    5. AWS Top Secret Region
      1. The AWS Top Secret Region launched in 2014 as the first air-gapped commercial cloud for the U.S. Intelligence Community
      2. "The Top Secret region is intended for the exclusive use of 18 government agencies comprising the USA intelligence community."
      3. "The CIA and AWS have partnered to create additional security overlays in addition to the air gap and three geographically dispersed zones of availability."
      4. "The Top Secret Region is hosted on-premise at the CIA, and is air gapped, completely separated from the public Internet for extra security."
      5. The top secret government data centers, e.g. US-ISOB-East-1
      6. Are part of the AWS-iso-b partition
      1. References:
        1. AWS GovCloud Services: Sensitive and Classified Data on the Public Cloud
        2. CIA's Cloud is 'Pretty Close' to Invincible, CIO Says
    6. AWS Secret Region
      1. The AWS Secret Region operates workloads up to the Secret U.S. security classification level.
      2. "The AWS Secret Region is used for sensitive, classified workloads by non-intelligence government organizations, and can be used by intelligence organizations to share data that is not top secret with other agencies."
      3. The Secret Region is available to all government agencies, not just intelligence agencies
      4. The AWS Secret Region addresses the full range of U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) data classifications, including Unclassified, Sensitive (CUI), Secret, and Top Secret.
      5. The Secret and Top Secret Regions, each consists of three Availability Zones.
      6. The secret government data centers, e.g. US-ISO-East-1
      7. Are part of the AWS-iso partition

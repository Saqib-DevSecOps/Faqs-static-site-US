1. What is Scaling Out and Scaling In? [AWS, Compute, Auto Scaling]
    1. The process of scaling out (or scaling in) involves changing system capacity by adding (or removing) discrete system units
    2. I.e. you can Scale Out by adding a second a EC2 Instance, providing that much more capacity for your workloads. Scaling In involves removing capacity by for example going from two to one EC2 Instance.
    3. This type of scaling (Scaling Out or Scaling In) is also known as Horizontal Scaling, and it does NOT involve having to restart the workload to either add capacity or remove capacity
    4. EC2 Auto Scaling does Horizontal Scaling, i.e. Scaling Out and Scaling In

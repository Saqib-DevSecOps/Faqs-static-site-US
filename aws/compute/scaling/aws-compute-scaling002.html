1. Can you automate Vertical Scaling on AWS? [AWS, Compute, Auto Scaling]
    1. Vertical Scaling, also known as Scaling Up and Scaling Down, is the process of increasing (scaling up), or decreasing (scaling down) the capacity of a single instance, or device. E.g. adding or removing memory or vCPU to a single EC2 Instance.
    2. In AWS the capacities of an EC2 Instance are pre-defined (at instance launch). Scaling up or down capacity for EC2 Instances involves resizing to the next defined size, or a different EC2 Instance, with the desired, pre-defined capacities.
    3. The AWS Ops Automator solutionÂ is an AWS Solutions Library product that now includes an extensive suite of actions that you can use to automate the management of snapshots, instances, and vertical scaling.
    4. "The solution can resize your instances by restarting your existing instance with a new size. Or, the solution can resize your instances by replacing your existing instance with a new, resized instance."

1. Any reason to still use Reserved Instance discount program? [AWS: Compute: Pricing]
    1. AWS Savings Plans at present only applies to EC2 Instances, Fargate, Lambda, SageMaker usage. "If your business is using Reserved Instances to save money on the cost of RDS Instances, AWS Redshift, ElastiCache, Elasticsearch, or DynamoDB services, you will have to continue using Reserved Instances until the new discount program is extended to these services."
    2. "You can get shorter term RIs on the marketplace"
    3. You get the same or slightly higher discounts with RI over Savings Plan.
    4. References:
      1. https://www.cloudhealthtech.com/blog/reserved-instances-vs-aws-saving-plans
      2. https://aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/

1. Will my Savings Plan renew automatically at the end of the 1 or 3 year term? [AWS: Compute: Pricing]
    1. No
    2. However you can queue your Savings Plan purchases for a specific date and time in the future
    3. As such, to make sure there is no gap in your savings, you can schedule the new Savings Plan term to begin just as the current/old term ends

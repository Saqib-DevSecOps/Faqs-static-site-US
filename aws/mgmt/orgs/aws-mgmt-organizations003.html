1. Can you have multiple Management Accounts in the root? [AWS: Management: AWS Organizations, Hierarchy, Accounts]
    1. With respect to AWS Organizations, there is only one Management account
    2. The Management account is the account that create the AWS Organizations and can optionally invite other accounts to be Member accounts
    3. Each Member account has its own AWS Root account, however they are under the purview of the one Management account, and subject to any Service Control Policy (SCP) applied by the Management account

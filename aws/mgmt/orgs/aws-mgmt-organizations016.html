1. In AWS Organizations, what are Member Accounts and Linked Accounts? [AWS: Management: AWS Organizations, Accounts]
    1. Member Accounts are the "invited" Accounts under control of the Master Account
    2. Linked Accounts are contextually similar to Member Accounts, sometimes used interchangeably, however also sometimes linked to AWS Organizations using the Consolidated Billing-only feature option.

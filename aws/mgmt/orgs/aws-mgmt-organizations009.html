1. Can you apply a Service Control Policy (SCP) at an individual member account level? [AWS: Management: AWS Organizations, Policy]
    1. Yes.
    2. An SCP can be applied to the Root, OU or individual account.
    3. An SCP applies at the entity and below.
      1. When you attach a policy to the organization root, all OUs and accounts in the organization inherit that policy.
      2. When you attach a policy to a specific OU, accounts that are directly under that OU or any child OU inherit the policy.
      3. When you attach a policy to a specific account, it affects only that account.
    4. Note: While it's attaching an SCP to a member account is allowed, carefully consider the use-case. It might be better to create an appropriate Organizational Unit (OU) for the account, and apply the SCP to the OU.

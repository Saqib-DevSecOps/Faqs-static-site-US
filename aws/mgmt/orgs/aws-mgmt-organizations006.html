1. Can a member account leave an Organization? [AWS: Management: AWS Organizations, Accounts]
    1. Yes
    2. Method 1: Remove an invited member account by signing in to the management account
      1. Sign-in to the management account and open the AWS Organizations console
      2. In the left pane, choose AWS accounts
      3. Click on the account you want to remove, then click Remove.
      4. Provide a valid payment method, if needed
    3. Method 2: Remove an invited member account by signing in to the member account
      1. Sign-in to the member account that you want to remove from the organization
      2. Navigate to the AWS Organizations console
      3. Choose Leave this organization
      1. Provide a valid payment method, if needed

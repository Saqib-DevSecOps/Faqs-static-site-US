1. Is there a difference between AWS Marketplace and Instance Marketplace? [AWS: AWS Marketplace: Information, Pricing]
    1. Instance Marketplace, are you referring to Reserved Instance (RI) Marketplace? A place to sell Standard Reserved Instances
    2. AWS freed customers from the burden of carry along Standard RI that no longer met their needs by allowing customer to re-sell them on the secondary marketplace, called the RI Marketplace.
      1. The other type of flexibility that AWS made available is that instead of, or in addition to, Standard RIs, customer can purchase Convertible RIs, which affords more flexibility than Standard RIs.
      2. Finally, AWS Savings Plan is the latest, and most flexible options customer have to commit to a term of use of EC2, Fargate, Lambda (with Compute Savings Plan), at a significant discount in return for the commitment.
      3. https://totalcloudio.medium.com/the-ultimate-guide-to-reserved-instance-and-aws-savings-plan-cf2b3c27bdc6
      4. https://aws.amazon.com/ec2/purchasing-options/reserved-instances/marketplace/
    3. Advantages of RI: You can sell unused RI in the RI Marketplace. You can get discounts for RDS, as well as EC2 with RIs.
    4. AWS Marketplace is the general marketplace for AWS services
